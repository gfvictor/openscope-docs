# Professional Experience

Different candidates have completely different life backgrounds. The form needs to adapt to the size of each person's professional baggage.

### What is it?

A modular form interface (Field Arrays) that allows the candidate to add or remove infinite individual blocks of "Professional Experiences", whether full-time, part-time, or otherwise, with just one click.

### What is it for?

Old systems limited the candidate to "3 spaces for previous jobs". The dynamic history serves to give freedom: a first-job candidate can completely delete the block, while a veteran can add 10 different blocks to detail their entire career.

### How is it used?

At the end of the form, there is a "Professional Experience" section:

1. The candidate fills in the predefined block with their experience or deletes the field if they don't have any.
2. If there is more than one experience, they click the **Add Professional Experience** button and fill in the new block.
3. If the candidate wants to remove an already filled block, they simply click the trash icon next to that specific block.

### Expected Result

A clean and non-intimidating interface. The form starts short, and only grows on demand if the candidate has more information to add, ensuring a much higher conversion rate (form completion and submission) from the candidates.
