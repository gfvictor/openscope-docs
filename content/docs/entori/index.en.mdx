# **EnTori** (Online Form)

**EnTori** is the digital front door of the agency. It modernizes the recruitment process by providing four fundamental benefits:

  <Card
    title="Data Standardization"
    description="Forces the structuring of information, eliminating illegible handwriting and missing data."
  />
  <Card
    title="Complete Capture"

  />
  <Card
    title="Digital Signature"
    description="Allows the candidate to digitally sign the legal consent terms via their mobile phone."
  />
  <Card

  />
</Cards>

### What is it?

It is a responsive web application (_PWA-ready_) focused exclusively on capturing Human Resources data. It replaces the old paper clipboard with a highly dynamic and secure digital form, where the candidate accesses the platform through a public link (or _QR Code_) on their own mobile device.

### What is it for?

The main objective of **EnTori** is to save hours of manual HR screening. Instead of the team deciphering printed forms and typing everything into the system, the candidate themselves inputs and validates their data. The result is an immediate digital resume, ready to be allocated to client companies or exported as a PDF/Excel.
