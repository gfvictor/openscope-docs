# EnTori (Online Form)

### What is it?

It is a responsive web application (_PWA-ready_) focused exclusively on capturing Human Resources data. It replaces the old paper clipboard with a highly dynamic and secure digital form, where all relevant information to the candidate's professional profile is captured.

### What is it for?

The main objective of EnTori is to standardize data entry. Instead of the HR team deciphering cursive handwriting or dealing with missing data on printed forms, **EnTori** forces the structuring of information sent by the candidate, validating it and making this information ready for the database.

### How is it used?

The candidate accesses the platform through a public link (or a _QR Code_ provided by the agency) on their own mobile device. They fill in their personal data, Japanese proficiency level, types of professional certifications, and, if any, past professional experiences. Before submitting the form, they digitally sign the consent terms.

### Expected Result

Once submitted, the form automatically drops into the **Rakudash** administrative dashboard, generating a clean, translated, and typo-free candidate profile, saving hours of manual HR screening. From this profile, the candidate's resume becomes available for the manager to hire them as an employee, allocate them to client companies, export their resume as a PDF or Excel, etc.
