# Access and Security

Rakudash is a restricted and secure environment, built specifically to protect the confidential data managed by the agency.

### What is it?

It is the authentication control and administrative profile management system. It isolates staff accounts from any external access, ensuring that candidates or employees (EnTori or WanSpot users) cannot view internal operations.

### What is it for?

To guarantee system integrity, prevent unauthorized access, and provide administrators with autonomy to update their own security credentials and display data.

### How is it used?

- **Logging into the System (Login):** Access is granted using your registered corporate email and your personal password. The initial email is always provided by the general administrator.
  > <span className="font-semibold">Session Timeout Notice:</span>
  > <span className="font-normal">
  >   For information security reasons, Rakudash automatically ends your session after a long period
  >   of inactivity. If the login screen is displayed while you are working, simply enter your
  >   credentials again to continue.
  > </span>
- **Password Recovery:** If you forget your password, use the recovery option available on the login screen. The system will send a secure and temporary link directly to your corporate email for resetting.
- **My Profile:** To manage your basic information without relying on technical support, click on your user inside the panel to open the Profile modal. Through it, you can update your **Display Name** and change your **Password**.

### Expected Result

A secure, autonomous, and traceable access, ensuring that the administrative operations of Rakudash are always protected against unauthorized viewing or external attacks.
