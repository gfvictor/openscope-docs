# Agency Properties

### What is it?

A dashboard focused on mapping and controlling the rented properties (apartments, houses, etc.) that the agency offers as accommodation for its employees.

### What is it for?

To centralize information about direct contracts with real estate agencies, manage the operational costs of each property (utility bills), and link these properties to the rental agreements of the occupying employees.

### How is it used?

The interface presents a list of operational properties containing:

- Property name;
- Rent amount paid to the real estate agency;
- Property type (e.g., 1DK, 2LDK, etc.);
- Prefecture and municipality;
- Start and end dates of the contract with the real estate agency.

Clicking on a property directs the manager to a management panel divided into two tabs:

1. **Property Information:**
   - **Basic Data:**
     - Complete address;
     - Architectural floor plan type (2DK, etc.);
     - Validity period (start and end) of the real estate contract;
     - Space for free operational notes.
   - **Employee Contract:**
     - Details of the rental agreement with the accommodated employee (visible if the property is occupied).

2. **Property Management:**
   - **Extra Costs:**
     - Interface for consolidating the property's operational expenses (electricity, gas, water, and similar).
   - **Real Estate Data:**
     - Registration information and commercial contacts regarding the direct contract with the real estate agency.

### Expected Result

Impeccable housing organization. The agency maintains total control over its rented assets, monitoring contract deadlines with real estate agencies and ensuring the correct allocation of costs to accommodated employees.
