# Exporting

### What is it?

A robust tool for extracting tabular data (data dump) regarding employee records and current allocations.

### What is it for?

To enable the strategic transfer of information to external systems, third-party accounting firms, or internal management audits, ensuring absolute flexibility in handling the agency's raw data.

### How is it used?

The interface presents segmented extraction options allocated in two operational cards:

1. **Allocation Export (By Company):**
   - Extraction of the employee list parameterized by the companies where they work.
   - Triggering the `Export List` action opens a configuration modal with the following options:
     - Filter by a specific agency office;
     - Selection of a target client company or global extraction (which generates individual spreadsheets for each client in the batch);
     - Delimitation of the desired validity period for the export;
     - Contractual status filter (exclusivity of active employees or inclusion of full history encompassing terminations).

2. **Global Export (Full Workforce):**
   - Massive extraction of the entire database of employees registered with the agency, regardless of allocation.
   - Triggering the `Export List` action only requires the manager to define the consolidation period in the modal.
   - The result is a unified file containing the complete demographic and contractual census of the corporation.

<Callout title="File Standard:">
  Aiming for compatibility and direct integration with market accounting software, all export
  routines package and provide reports exclusively in Microsoft Excel (.xlsx) format.
</Callout>

### Expected Result

Unrestricted data mobility. The agency maintains sovereignty over its corporate information, allowing for the rapid, frictionless extraction of complex reports, which are essential for timely decision-making and accountability.
