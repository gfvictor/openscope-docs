# Administration

### What is it?

The bureaucratic and managerial core of Rakudash, focused on auditing, internal processes, protocols, and all the documentation that makes up the agency's corporate "paperwork."

### What is it for?

To separate structural business management (organization of physical branches and client companies) from people control (Human Resources and Requests). It ensures that B2B contractual rules, billing, and the office organizational chart operate in an isolated and secure manner.

### How is it used?

The dashboard provides access to the agency's structural tools. Through the sidebar navigation, the manager accesses and manages the registration and business rules of **Offices** (headquarters and branches) and **Client Companies** (factories and contractors).

### Expected Result

Impeccable corporate organization. The agency has full traceability of its physical operations and client relationships (B2B), ensuring that billing, specific sector rules, and branch hierarchies function perfectly.
