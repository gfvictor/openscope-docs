# Paid Vacations

### What is it?

An administrative dashboard dedicated to planning and controlling the granting of paid vacation days (Yūkyū Kyūka) acquired by employees.

### What is it for?

To monitor the acquisition cycles of vacation rights, ensuring that the employee and the agency operate in full compliance with Japanese labor legislation. It serves to automate the tracking of expiration dates, preventing the agency from suffering sanctions for not granting the mandatory days within the legally stipulated time frame.

### How is it used?

The master list displays the consolidated vacation scenario of the workforce. The exposed data includes:

- Employee's name;
- Formal hiring date;
- Contractual situation (active in allocation, waiting, or in termination process);
- Current allocated client company;
- Date of the last vacation grant;
- Forecast of the next grant and impending expiration date;
- Total days granted and volume of days used to date.

To insert a new vacation cycle, the manager executes the following flow:

1. **Manual Scheduling (New Record):**
   - Opening the granting modal;
   - Selection of the target employee;
   - Insertion of the exact grant date;
   - Definition of the volume of days to be credited, conditioned to the length of service and labor legislation requirements (Yūkyū Kyūka).

### Expected Result

Absolute governance and compliance. The agency never misses expiration dates, manages the distribution of benefits in a strictly legal manner, and prevents fines or labor liabilities.
