# Candidates

The gateway to the agency. This menu concentrates all individuals who have shown interest in working with you.

### What is it?

A list of all individuals who have filled out the digital resume via EnTori, but who have not yet undergone any interview or formal approval process.

### What is it for?

It functions as the "inbox" for the recruitment sector. It is where HR evaluates profiles, makes contact to schedule interviews, and decides whether to move forward with the candidate.

### How is it used?

Upon accessing the **Candidates** menu, the manager views a table with names, photos, and basic contact data of the newcomers.
By clicking on a specific candidate, the full profile containing the resume filled out in EnTori opens up. From there, the manager can:

- **Approve:** Move the candidate to the "Waiting for Hire" queue.
- **Reject:** Discard the candidate (moving them to the archive).
- **Add Notes:** Register observations about the phone or in-person interview.

### Expected Result

No talent lost. HR can clear the queue daily, ensuring that all interested parties receive quick feedback and suitable profiles are rapidly directed to hiring.
