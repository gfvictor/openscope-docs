# Personal Information

The initial tab of the candidate's profile provides a complete view of the submitted resume and decision-making tools for HR.

### What is it?

A dual panel that displays the individual's essential registration data on one side and the qualification history on the other, while also gathering the control buttons for the selection process.

### What is it for?

To centralize candidate evaluation. This is where the recruiter analyzes if the profile meets the job requirements and executes the approval, rejection, or generation of the formatted resume to present to the client (factory).

### How is it used?

The interface is divided into two main cards:

1. **Personal Information:**
   - Displays photo, name, and furigana.
   - Nationality, visa type, visa expiration, birth date, and marital status.
   - Japanese proficiency level (following the EnTori standard).
   - Full address, phone, email, and bank details.
   - Information for the creation and authorization of WanSpot access credentials.
   - Buttons to change the application status (Approve or Reject) and to generate and export the resume in Excel or PDF using the registered Templates.

2. **Licenses and Certificates:**
   - Lists all licenses and certificates reported by the candidate during the EnTori registration.
   - The manager can add new certificates, or edit and delete existing ones, keeping the qualification history perfectly up to date.

### Expected Result

Efficient screening with no extra paperwork. The manager has all the information clearly laid out and, with a single click, can approve the candidate or generate the formatted resume to send to the client.
