# Allocation Status

Complete control of the employee's dispatch and work history.

### What is it?

A detailed dashboard that gathers all information regarding the employee's dispatch (work allocation), contracts, insurance, and movement history across client companies.

### What is it for?

It keeps the manager perfectly informed about where the employee is working, under what contractual terms, and their trajectory within the agency since admission.

### How is it used?

The page features several separated information cards:

1. **Enrollment and Dispatch Status:**
   - Allocation type;
   - Acceptance date at the company;
   - Target allocation and sector;
   - Contract start and end dates;
   - Amount paid by the client company to the agency.

2. **Social and Unemployment Insurance:**
   - Social Insurance (Shakai Hoken) information;
   - Unemployment Insurance information.

3. **Histories:**
   - Candidacy and client allocation history;
   - Allocation transfer history;
   - Termination history;
   - General work history.

### Expected Result

Provides a 360º view of the employee's entire professional and contractual life, enabling quick decisions and responses about the dispatch situation at any time.
