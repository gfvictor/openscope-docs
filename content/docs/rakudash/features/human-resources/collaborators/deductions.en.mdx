# Deductions

Management of salary advances and rent payroll deductions.

### What is it?

The employee's financial hub for the continuous recording of expenses and advances that will need to be deducted during the final salary calculation.

### What is it for?

Ensures the agency has no gaps or losses when giving financial cash advances to employees and facilitates the automatic collection of rent when the employee resides in a company-provided apartment.

### How is it used?

The interface focuses exclusively on the employee's debts to the company through two sections:

1. **Contract and Rent Information:**
   - Identification of current corporate housing;
   - Exact monthly deduction amount for rent.

2. **Salary Advances:**
   - Specific date of advance concession;
   - Exact monetary value of the advance;
   - Tracking for correct payroll deduction.

### Expected Result

Prevents crucial human errors and oversights during payroll closing by centralizing the control of financial outflows.
