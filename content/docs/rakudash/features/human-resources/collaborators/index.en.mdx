# Collaborators

Where daily operations happen. Here you will find the company's effective workforce.

### What is it?

The official and general roster of active employees. Displays all individuals who have already signed a contract and are currently working at a client company (destination allocation).

### What is it for?

It serves as the main database to manage the people who generate revenue for the agency. It allows you to quickly check where each person is working, their bank details, dependents, and history.

### How is it used?

In the **Collaborators** menu, the table lists active employees with quick search shortcuts.
When opening a collaborator's profile, the manager has access to:

- **Personal Data and Documents:** Copy of the zairyu card, passport, etc.
- **Contracts and Allocation:** The factory where they currently work and the start and end dates.
- **Movement Actions:** Buttons to initiate a "Transfer" (change factories) or a "Termination" (start the firing/resignation process).

### Expected Result

A command center where the manager finds any document or information about an active employee in less than three clicks, facilitating daily problem-solving.
