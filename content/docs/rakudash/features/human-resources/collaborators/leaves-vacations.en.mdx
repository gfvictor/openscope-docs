# Leaves and Paid Vacations

Control and audit panel for Yukyu (paid vacations).

### What is it?

The section dedicated to controlling absences and, mainly, the management of the paid vacation balance guaranteed by Japanese labor laws (Yukyu).

### What is it for?

Ensures the agency is in full compliance with labor legislation, meticulously tracking how many rest days the employee is entitled to and their usage history.

### How is it used?

The screen is divided into two main panels:

1. **Vacation Management:**
   - Manual addition of extra days;
   - Balance editing;
   - Deletion of days;
   - Automatic calculation by the system based on hiring.

2. **Usage History:**
   - Exact dates of usage;
   - Amount of days consumed.

### Expected Result

Total transparency in the management of days off, avoiding labor disputes and ensuring the employee receives their rights correctly.
