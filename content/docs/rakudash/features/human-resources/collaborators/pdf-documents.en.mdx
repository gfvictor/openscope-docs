# PDF Documents

The official repository for contracts, payslips, and employee files.

### What is it?

A secure and restricted digital vault dedicated exclusively to documents that need to be generated, exported, or archived regarding the employee's life.

### What is it for?

To keep bureaucratic documents (such as employment contracts and monthly payslips) organized and secure. Document editing is restricted to the manager in Rakudash (employees cannot upload files directly via WanSpot) to prevent digital clutter and ensure corporate security.

### How is it used?

In this area, the manager has access to:

1. **Exportable Documents:**
   - Generation of employment contracts;
   - Viewing and exporting of payslips.

2. **Addition of Documents:**
   - Manual upload of official PDFs by the manager via Rakudash;
   - Total block of direct uploads by the employee via WanSpot.

### Expected Result

A 100% official and auditable digital dossier, keeping the paperwork history secure, organized, and free of digital clutter.
