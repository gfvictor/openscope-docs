# Human Resources

The Human Resources module is the operational heart of the agency, tracking the journey of each individual from the moment they register until their eventual termination.

### What is it?

A centralized panel that categorizes and organizes all individual profiles registered in the system, classifying them according to their current phase in the company's lifecycle.

### What is it for?

To allow the HR department to have full control over the hiring and allocation pipeline, preventing candidates from getting lost in the process or collaborators with pending transfers from being forgotten.

### How is it used?

In the sidebar menu, the Human Resources section acts as a process pipeline, divided into 5 different work queues. Navigate through the subtopics on the side to understand in detail how to manage each stage:

- **Candidates**
- **Collaborators**
- **Waiting for Hire**
- **Waiting for Transfer**
- **Waiting for Termination**

### Expected Result

Fluid, bottleneck-free people management, where managers know exactly how many individuals are in each stage of the process and what actions need to be taken to keep the department moving.
