# Waiting for Hire

The bureaucratic transition phase. They have already been approved, but the official paperwork is still missing.

### What is it?

An exclusive queue for candidates who have already passed interviews, but who are still waiting for the issuance of the official contract or the exact definition of which factory (destination allocation) they will be sent to.

### What is it for?

To prevent approved candidates from being stuck in "limbo" waiting for feedback, ensuring that HR concludes the bureaucratic process (contract signing, medical exams, etc.) as quickly as possible.

### How is it used?

Upon entering the **Waiting for Hire** menu, the manager views the approved profiles and can:

- Define the **Destination Allocation** (the factory where the person will work).
- Generate and issue **Employment Contracts** and admission documents using the Templates module.
- Mark the process as completed, which will automatically move the individual to the official list of **Collaborators**.

### Expected Result

Agility in admission. The candidate doesn't wait days for an answer after being approved, and the agency ensures they start generating value quickly.
