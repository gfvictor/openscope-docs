# Waiting for Termination

Closing the cycle. An employee has left, but administrative pending items still exist.

### What is it?

A queue of collaborators who resigned or were fired, but whose departure process has not yet been completely finalized in the system.

### What is it for?

To ensure the agency does not suffer financial losses by forgetting to collect on pending items (such as apartment cleaning, uniform return, or salary advance payoff) before paying the last salary.

### How is it used?

Upon triggering the termination of a collaborator, their profile is frozen on this screen.
The HR or Financial manager accesses this menu to go through the departure _checklist_:

- Check if the employee has already vacated the housing (Agency Housing).
- Confirm the return of agency items.
- Perform the severance calculation.
  Only after "Ticking" all these pending items does the manager mark the termination as completed, permanently archiving the profile and freeing up slots (in housing and allocation).

<Callout title="Attention to Paid Vacations:">
  If the collaborator has unused paid vacation days, the system will block the termination. The manager must ensure that these vacations are used by the collaborator before the effective departure date, or manually delete the balance through the panel.
</Callout>

### Expected Result

Legal and financial security during the termination process. The agency eliminates the risk of liabilities and losses arising from unsettled pending items, ensuring that the entire administrative and bureaucratic flow of the severance is strictly fulfilled and auditable.
