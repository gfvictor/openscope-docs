# Waiting for Transfer

The most critical moment for talent retention: moving from one client to another.

### What is it?

A dedicated list of active collaborators who are in the process of changing allocations (leaving one factory to enter another).

### What is it for?

To manage the "gap" (waiting time) between contracts. It is crucial so that the employee does not go weeks without getting paid and end up looking for another agency due to lack of assistance.

### How is it used?

The employee falls into this list as soon as the manager finalizes the contract at the current factory, but indicates that the collaborator will stay with the agency.
Within this menu, HR must act quickly to:

- Find a suitable new **Destination Allocation**.
- Issue a new employment contract for the new factory.
- Finalize the transfer, returning the employee to normal status within the **Collaborators** roster.

### Expected Result

Optimization of talent retention by minimizing idle periods between contracts. The agency gains predictive and monitoring capabilities to reallocate professionals strategically, mitigating the risk of voluntary turnover due to a lack of placements.
