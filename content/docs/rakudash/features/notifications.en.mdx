# Notifications

Rakudash's notification system ensures real-time monitoring of agency operations, guaranteeing that managers are instantly informed about critical actions and requests.

### What is it?

A real-time messaging engine integrated with a Push notification service that keeps the manager connected to everything happening in the ecosystem without needing to refresh the page.

### What is it for?

To avoid depending on phone calls or text messages to track daily processes. It warns the manager immediately when an employee makes a request on WanSpot or when a new candidate finishes registering a resume on EnTori.

### How is it used?

The alert center acts both passively and actively in your daily routine:

- **The Bell:** Located at the top corner of the screen. By clicking it, you can view the history of recent alerts.
- **Real-Time:** If you have Rakudash open on your screen, new alerts will not generate intrusive pop-ups. Instead, the system will instantly show a red badge with the number of unread notifications over the Bell, without you needing to press F5 or reload the tab.
- **Push Notifications on Mobile:** Critical notices (such as the entry of a new candidate) are sent directly to your phone's or desktop's lock screen, allowing immediate action.
  <Callout title="Mandatory Installation (PWA):">
    To receive alerts on the lock screen with the system closed, the Rakudash system must be
    previously installed on the device as an application.
  </Callout>

### Expected Result

A highly responsive management team capable of acting within seconds after a leave request, a salary advance request, or a new recruitment, closing the company's communication loop perfectly.
