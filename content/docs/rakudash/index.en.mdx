### What is it?

It is a high-performance Administrative Panel (ERP/CRM) built exclusively for the agency's daily operations. Unlike the other systems in the ecosystem, access to Rakudash is **100% restricted**, strictly allowing access only to internal staff (managers, HR, and finance).

### What is it for?

The main objective of **Rakudash** is to eradicate the chaos of separate spreadsheets and lost papers. It acts as the company's single point of truth, where the manager can track metrics in real-time on the Dashboard, approve employee requests with one click, and export exact invoices at the end of the month with no room for human error.
