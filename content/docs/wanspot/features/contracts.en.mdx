# Official Contracts

Continuous transparency and auditability of established labor and housing conditions.

### What is it?

An immutable, read-only digital repository that stores the complete history of Employment Contracts (Koyou Keiyakusho) and Corporate Housing Lease Agreements linked to the employee.

### What is it for?

To decentralize access to the legal documentation of the employment relationship. It mitigates risks of labor liabilities due to misinformation, eliminating the reliance on physical copies and in-person visits to the agency's office.

### How is it used?

Through the **Files** menu, under the **Contracts** option, the employee accesses their repository of current and historical contracts. The interface allows autonomous and immediate consultation of vital data such as hourly wage, bonus structure, agreed working hours, as well as terms and deductions applicable to corporate housing.

### Expected Result

Guarantee of compliance and mutual legal security. The employee gains legal protection by having their terms always at hand, while the agency substantially reduces operational support related to questions about working hours and housing deductions.
