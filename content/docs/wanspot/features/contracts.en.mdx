# Official Contracts

Total transparency and unrestricted access to the conditions agreed upon between the agency and the employee.

### What is it?

An immutable digital repository that lists not only the employment contracts but also real estate lease contracts (if the agency provides housing and the employee is renting).

### What is it for?

To eliminate the employee's dependence on having to go to the agency's office to get paper copies or having doubts about what was agreed. It serves to give them clarity about their working and housing conditions at any time.

### How is it used?

In the `/contracts` section, the employee can open their current and past contracts. They can independently check their hourly wage, bonuses, stipulated working hours, and the terms and deductions of their rent.

### Expected Result

The employee feels more secure and valued having their contracts always at hand. The agency ensures legal compliance and drastically reduces repetitive doubts about payment and housing rules.
