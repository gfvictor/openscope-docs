# Document Hub

Official one-way communication and distribution of essential bureaucratic documentation.

### What is it?

A secure digital library where the agency makes essential files and company policies available exclusively for reading and downloading by the employee.

### What is it for?

To serve as the official channel for bureaucratic notices. The agency needs to ensure that critical newsletters or official documents (such as the annual income statement, the Gensen Choshuhyo) reach the employee safely and privately, without the risks of WhatsApp.

### How is it used?

HR uploads the files directly to the employee's profile (or globally) via **Rakudash**. The employee accesses the `/documents` route on **WanSpot**, where they can read announcements, access general company files, or save their tax files.

### Expected Result

Guarantee that sensitive files or mass announcements are delivered digitally in a secure environment, unifying company communication and preventing important information from getting lost over time.
