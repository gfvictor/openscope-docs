# Personal Documents

Centralized portal for electronic document management and corporate announcements.

### What is it?

A two-way Document Management System where the agency provisions institutional and operational files, while simultaneously enabling the collection of sensitive documentation from employees.

### What is it for?

To extinguish the traffic of sensitive data through informal channels (like WhatsApp) and optimize physical mailing costs. It enables the structured delivery of income reports and the secure collection of Passport and residence card copies.

### How is it used?

The Human Resources department performs the upload and provisioning of files directly to the employee's profile (or through global broadcasts) via **Rakudash**. Through **WanSpot**, the employee navigates to the **Files** menu and selects the **Documents** option, having immediate access to corporate announcements, company instructional materials, and pertinent tax documentation (such as the Gensen).

### Expected Result

Establishment of an official, secure, and traceable channel. The agency consolidates its critical communications in a controlled environment, shielding the operation against data loss and protecting the employee's confidential information.
