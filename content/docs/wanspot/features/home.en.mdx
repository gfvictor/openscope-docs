# Initial Dashboard

Consolidated access to the employee's metrics and contractual status immediately after authentication.

### What is it?

An employee-focused executive dashboard interface presenting their complete profile, current allocation status, and key indicators of their journey at the agency.

### What is it for?

To provide absolute transparency regarding the employee's registration and employment situation. It eliminates basic communication friction, allowing the employee to audit their own data and status without the need for Human Resources intervention.

### How is it used?

Upon authenticating to the platform, the employee has immediate access to the following data on the dashboard:

- **Personal Information:** Full name, date of birth, and updated address in the system.
- **Allocation:** Client company where they are allocated and the operation start date at the respective factory.
- **Professional History:** Official admission date at the agency.
- **Paid Vacations (Yukyu):** Consolidated and updated balance of days available for paid vacation leave.

### Expected Result

Drastic reduction in the volume of calls to HR for trivial inquiries (such as vacation balance or address confirmation). The employee gains full confidence in the agency's compliance through continuous and transparent visibility of their data.
