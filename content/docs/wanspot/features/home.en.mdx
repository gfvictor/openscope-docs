# Initial Dashboard

As soon as the employee logs into **WanSpot**, they don't need to navigate to know their situation. The main screen delivers everything they need to know at a glance.

### What is it?

A summary page containing the employee's complete profile and their client allocation data.

### What is it for?

It serves to keep the employee aware that the agency has their correct details, as well as to quickly answer questions about their own current employment status.

### How is it used?

Upon opening the home page, the employee has immediate access to the following data:

- **Personal Information:** Full name, date of birth with exact calculated age, and current address.
- **Allocation:** Name of the client company where they are currently working and the start date at that factory.
- **Agency History:** Official hire date at the agency.
- **Paid Leave (Yukyu):** The exact current balance of available days to take paid leave.

### Expected Result

The employee gains peace of mind by seeing that the agency keeps their registration and allocation data correct and up-to-date, and HR stops receiving daily questions about "how many Yukyu days do I have?".
