# Leave and Absence Management

Documenting time and formalizing communication between the employee, the agency, and the client factory.

### What is it?

A scheduling and notification interface dedicated to recording the days the employee will not be present at work.

### What is it for?

To eliminate fragmented and informal communication. When an employee needs to be absent (Yasumi) or wishes to use their paid leave days (Yukyu), a simple word-of-mouth notice or loose message can get lost, causing problems with the factory.

### How is it used?

Through the `/leaves` tab, the employee fills in the dates they need to be absent and categorizes the reason for the absence. The request is formalized in the system, which alerts agency managers in a timely manner so they can pass the information to the client factory or process the Yukyu payment.
