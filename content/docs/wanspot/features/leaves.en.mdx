# Leave and Absence Management

Governance of attendance and traceability of absences (Yasumi) and paid vacations (Yukyu).

### What is it?

A transactional module focused on the registration, categorization, and approval workflow of scheduled or unforeseen absences.

### What is it for?

To mitigate factory contingent alignment failures caused by informal communications. Centralization formalizes requests for paid vacations (Yukyu) and absences (Yasumi), ensuring that information reaches managers without bottlenecks or data loss.

### How is it used?

Through the main menu, in the **Leaves** tab, the employee enters the dates of scheduled absence and categorizes the justification for the absence. The request is instantly formalized in the system, triggering timely alerts to the agency's managers. This ensures sufficient time to realign personnel with the client factory or properly process vacation pay (Yukyu).
