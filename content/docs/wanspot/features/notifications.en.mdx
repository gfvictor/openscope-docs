# Notifications and Alerts

Proactive push messaging to keep the employee synchronized with backoffice operations.

### What is it?

A centralized transactional alert engine natively integrated into **WanSpot**, developed to asynchronously notify the employee about status changes and critical events regarding their allocation.

### What is it for?

To eradicate the volume of calls to operational support driven by anxiety ("was my advance approved?", "were my vacations registered?"). The system takes on the responsibility of keeping the employee equipped with procedural information as soon as actions are validated by the agency.

### How is it used?

Alerts are automatically triggered by the system, delivering immediate notifications regarding:

- **Salary Advances:** Instant feedback of approval or refusal by the financial department.
- **Leaves and Paid Vacations:** Protocol confirmation and deliberation on absence records and use of paid vacations.
- **Paid Vacation Balance Renewal:** Automatic trigger upon the addition of legal batches of annual paid vacations.
- **Contracts and Documents:** Compulsory notice whenever updated contracts, vital announcements, or tax files are attached to the employee's dossier.

### Expected Result

Massive reduction in the Human Resources team's workload. The self-service cycle reaches operational fullness with transparent, auditable, and instantaneous communication, ensuring predictability for the employee.
