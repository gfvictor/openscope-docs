# Notifications and Alerts

Proactive communication to keep the employee always informed, without them having to constantly open the app to check for updates.

### What is it?

A real-time messaging and alert system integrated into the **WanSpot** dashboard that notifies the employee about critical events related to their employment with the agency.

### What is it for?

To eliminate employee anxiety and the excess of messages to HR support asking "was my advance approved?" or "were my vacation days accepted?". The system does the job of passively warning the employee as soon as any action is taken by the administrators.

### How is it used?

Notifications are triggered automatically, and the employee receives real-time alerts about:

- **Salary Advances:** Immediate notices when a salary advance request is approved or denied by HR.
- **Leaves and Yukyus:** Confirmation of receipt and approval of absence notices or use of paid leave.
- **Yukyu Batch Updates:** Automatic alert on the day a new batch of paid vacation days is credited to the employee's account.
- **Contracts and Documents:** Notification whenever a new employment contract is issued or an important document is made available by the agency.

### Expected Result

The employee feels supported and well-informed, as the platform actively updates them on every step of their requests, closing the self-service cycle with impeccable communication.
