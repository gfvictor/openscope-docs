# Digital Payrolls

The financial life of a employee in Japan is based on predictability and organization. **WanSpot** delivers absolute control and transparency over payments.

### What is it?

An area of the portal dedicated exclusively to viewing the financial files and data for each month worked.

### What is it for?

In the traditional model, HR prints dozens of payslips and delivers them to the factory, or wastes time generating and sending PDFs one by one via chat. This module automates this process, granting the employee autonomy to access their own payment receipts.

### How is it used?

As soon as accounting closes the payroll in **Rakudash**, the payslip is instantly released in the `/payrolls` tab on **WanSpot**. The employee clicks on the desired month, can view the breakdown of hours, additions, deductions, and download the PDF document at any time.

### Expected Result

Total decentralization of payslip distribution. The process that previously took days of printing, organization, and delivery now happens instantly, with zero cost and total data security.
