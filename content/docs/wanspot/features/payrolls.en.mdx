# Digital Payslips

Accounting digitization and automated delivery of salary statements.

### What is it?

A self-service financial module focused on the custody and structured presentation of monthly payslips.

### What is it for?

To eradicate the logistical overhead of the HR and financial departments. It replaces mass printing, sealing, and in-person delivery at factories (or manual sending of PDFs) with an automated payroll provisioning system.

### How is it used?

As soon as accounting closes the payroll in **Rakudash**, the payslip is instantly made available on **WanSpot**. By accessing the **Files** menu and selecting the **Payrolls** option, the employee selects the reference month to view the complete breakdown of hours worked, additions, and deductions, being able to download the PDF document at any time for their personal records.

### Expected Result

Total automation in payslip distribution. A process that traditionally consumed days of printing and logistics now happens instantly, with absolute traceability and compliance with data privacy laws.
