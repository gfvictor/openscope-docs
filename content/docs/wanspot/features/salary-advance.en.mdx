# Salary Advance

On-demand liquidity control with native integration to payroll.

### What is it?

A financial self-service suite that allows employees to request salary advances subject to pre-established business rules and operational ceilings.

### What is it for?

To eradicate informality and fragmentation in the process of granting labor credit. It eliminates requests via messaging apps, protecting the financial department from liabilities due to improper deductions and cash shortages.

### How is it used?

The employee accesses the **Salary Advances** tab, enters the desired withdrawal amount (strictly respecting the operational ceilings stipulated by the agency) and submits the request. The request is routed in a structured manner to the **Rakudash** administrative dashboard, awaiting evaluation, approval, or refusal by the financial department.

Consolidation of a traceable and auditable financial flow from end to end. The process gains scale and accounting rigor, and deductions are automatically provisioned at the **Rakudash** payroll closing.
