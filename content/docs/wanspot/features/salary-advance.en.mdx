# Salary Advance

Ensuring liquidity for the employee in times of need, without adding bureaucracy to the finance department.

### What is it?

An integrated self-service system for the employee to request withdrawals or advances from their own salary before the official payday.

### What is it for?

To automate one of the most recurring and chaotic processes of employment agencies in Japan. Requests scattered across WhatsApp make financial management and auditing difficult, generating errors in future deductions.

### How is it used?

The employee accesses the `/salary-advance` route, enters the amount they wish to advance (respecting the limit stipulated by the agency) and sends the request. The request goes in a structured way to the **Rakudash** administrative dashboard so that HR can evaluate, approve, or deny it.

### Expected Result

Advance requests become centralized, organized, traceable, and much faster to process. The agency maintains an auditable history of every yen transferred, and the deduction is automatically scheduled in the payroll.
