# **WanSpot** (Employee Portal)

**WanSpot** is the employee's command center. It provides four fundamental pillars for the employee experience and agency operations:

<Cards>
  <Card
    title="Full Transparency"
    description="Exact access to paid leave balance, allocation history, and current contract rules."
  />
  <Card
    title="Financial Independence"
    description="Real-time viewing of payrolls and automated salary advance requests."
  />
  <Card
    title="Document Center"
    description="Secure access to newsletters and official files (such as employment and lease contracts) provided by the agency."
  />
  <Card
    title="Streamlined Communication"
    description="Leave and absence notices formally registered in the system, without relying on chat apps."
  />
</Cards>

---

### What is it?

It is an exclusive self-service web application (PWA) for active employees. It acts as a direct, uninterrupted bridge between the employee's mobile phone and the agency's database.

### What is it for?

The main goal of **WanSpot** is to grant autonomy to the employee and, consequently, reduce the flow of repetitive HR requests. Instead of overloading the agency by asking for payroll PDFs or paid leave balances, the employee checks and resolves everything through the app, 24/7.
